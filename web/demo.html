üõ†Ô∏è Annotation Workflow

  1. Upload Image: Drag satellite image to upload area
  2. Select Tool: Choose AI Point for automatic segmentation or Polygon for manual drawing
  3. Annotate:
    • AI Point: Click on features to generate segmentation
    • Polygon: Click to place points, double-click or right-click to finish
  4. Label: Add descriptive labels to your annotations
  5. Manage: Edit, delete, or select annotations from the sidebar
  6. Export: Download annotations as JSON file
